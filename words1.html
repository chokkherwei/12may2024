Xk (homework from EBC) : Tribute to Mummy dearest
My mummy is my hero.
My mummy is very loving. She puts her kids at her priority, One time when I was in kindergarten, my mum fell sick. She lost her voice, and went to lie down on the bed immediately after we had arrived at hime. Nevertheless, falling sick was not her excuse for being late. As always, she still came to pick me up from school on time. She didn't even forget to make milk or dinner for me. Although at that time, I found it a teeny weeny bit annoying that my mummy had to keep summoning me to do work, I still did it. I was heartbroken for her. When we get sick, we have our mummy beside us, but my mummy can only see her own mom once or twice a year. :(
